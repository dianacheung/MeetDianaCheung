Problem solving by way of:

  1. Find out as much as possible about the problem at hand - such as research, competitive analysis, and user testing.
  2. From the information, come up with alternatives or hypotheses.
  3. Determine evaluation criteria - such as pros and cons or scoring matrix.
  4. Decide and note success measures.
  5. Proceed and re-evaluate.
  6. Repeat.
